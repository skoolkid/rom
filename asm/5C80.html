5C80: PR-CC - Address of next position for LPRINT to print at
Read by the routine at PO_FETCH, and updated by the routines at PO_STORE and CLEAR_PRB. The first byte of this system variable is accessed by the routine at CLEAR_PRB via the IY register at IY+$46.
PR_CC 5C80 DEFW $0000
