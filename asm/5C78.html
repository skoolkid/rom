5C78: FRAMES - Frame counter
Read by the routine at RANDOMIZE, and updated by the routine at MASK_INT. The third byte of this system variable is accessed by the routine at MASK_INT via the IY register at IY+$40.
FRAMES 5C78 DEFB $00,$00,$00
