5C67: BREG - Calculator's B register
Read by the routines at PO_SCR and fp_calc_2, and updated by the routines at OUT_LINE, CALCULATE and dec_jr_nz. This system variable is accessed by some routines via the IY register at IY+$2D.
BREG 5C67 DEFB $00
