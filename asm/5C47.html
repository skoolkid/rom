5C47: SUBPPC - Number within line of statement being executed
Read by the routines at FOR and GO_SUB, and updated by the routines at MAIN_EXEC, LINE_SCAN, STMT_LOOP and NEXT_LINE. This system variable is accessed by some routines via the IY register at IY+$0D.
SUBPPC 5C47 DEFB $00
