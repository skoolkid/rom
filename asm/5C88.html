5C88: S-POSN - Column and line number for PRINT position
Read by the routines at PO_FETCH, PO_SCR, MAIN_EXEC and LIST, and updated by the routines at PO_STORE and INPUT. The second byte of this system variable (the line number) is accessed by some routines via the IY register at IY+$4F.
S_POSN 5C88 DEFB $00,$00
