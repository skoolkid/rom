5C0E: TVDATA - Colour, AT and TAB controls going to television
Used by the routine at PO_TV_2. When a control character (codes +10 to +17) and its operand(s) are being processed, these two bytes are used as temporary storage for the character code and its first operand.
TVDATA 5C0E DEFB $00,$00
