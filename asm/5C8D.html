5C8D: ATTR-P - Permanent current colours
Initialised by the routine at NEW, read by the routines at TEMPS, CLS and CL_LINE, and updated by the routine at CLASS_07. Holds the attribute byte that is used for the main part of the display when the screen is cleared.
ATTR_P 5C8D DEFB $38 FLASH 0: BRIGHT 0: PAPER 7: INK 0
