5C41: MODE - Specifies K, L, C, E or G cursor
Read by the routines at KEYBOARD and OUT_CURS, and updated by the routines at EDITOR, CLEAR_SP and KEY_INPUT. This system variable is accessed by some routines via the IY register at IY+$07. It holds one of the following values:
Value Cursor
0 C, K or L
1 E
2 G
Input
Output
MODE 5C41 DEFB $00
