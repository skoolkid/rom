23643: K-CUR - Address of cursor
Read by the routine at OUT_CURS, and updated by the routines at EDITOR, ED_EDIT, ED_RIGHT, CLEAR_SP, POINTERS, SET_MIN, INPUT and str. The second byte of this system variable is accessed by the routine at INPUT via the IY register at IY+34.
