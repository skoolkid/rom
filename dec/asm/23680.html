23680: PR-CC - Address of next position for LPRINT to print at
Read by the routine at PO_FETCH, and updated by the routines at PO_STORE and CLEAR_PRB. The first byte of this system variable is accessed by the routine at CLEAR_PRB via the IY register at IY+70.
Input
Output
PR_CC 23680 DEFW 0
