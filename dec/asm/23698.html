23698: MEMBOT - Calculator's memory area
Used by the routines at BEEP, PO_ANY, CIRCLE, e_to_fp and PRINT_FP. The 11th, 24th, 26th and 27th bytes of this area are accessed by some routines via the IY register at IY+98, IY+111, IY+113 and IY+114.
