23625: E-PPC - Number of current line
Read by the routines at ED_DOWN, ED_UP, AUTO_LIST and OUT_LINE, and updated by the routines at ED_EDIT, MAIN_ADD and LIST. The first byte of this system variable (the LSB) is accessed by the routine at ED_EDIT via the IY register at IY+15.
E_PPC 23625 DEFW 0
