23647: X-PTR - Address of the character after the '?' marker
Read by the routine at OUT_LINE, and updated by the routines at ERROR_1, LD_CONTRL, ME_ENTER, ED_COPY, MAIN_EXEC, POINTERS, READ_3 and INPUT. The second byte of this system variable is accessed by some routines via the IY register at IY+38.
