23624: BORDCR - Border colour
Initialised by the routine at NEW, read by the routines at BEEPER, SA_LD_RET, TEMPS (via the IY register at IY+14) and CL_LINE, and updated by the routine at BORDER. The border colour (0-7) is stored in bits 3-5.
