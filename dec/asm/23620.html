23620: NSPPC - Statement number in line to be jumped to
Read by the routines at STMT_RET, LINE_RUN and LINE_NEW, and updated by the routines at LD_CONTRL, MAIN_EXEC, NEXT_LINE, FOR and GO_TO. This system variable is accessed by some routines via the IY register at IY+10.
